Maker of Interlude

Interlude is an open-source keyboard VSRG client I've been working on as a hobby project since 2018.

Motivation:
- Consolidate my favourite features and charts from multiple similar VSRGs
- Creative freedom to try some of my own ideas out
- Explore my interest in game development

Make yourself at home

  • Import your songs, scores and skins from osu!mania
  • Import your songs from Etterna
  • Tools for getting offset and scroll speed right

Ease of use

  • Ingame downloads for songs, noteskins and more
  • Edit your noteskin ingame
  • Built in tooltips and wiki

Just play

  • All your songs in one place
  • Intelligent suggestions on what to play next
  • 'Endless mode' system to play back-to-back

Like what you see?

Interlude can only grow to its full potential with your help.
